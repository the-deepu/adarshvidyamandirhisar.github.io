Extracurricular activities are utmost important in a student's life. Students who get involved in extra activities meet new people and because of that their social skills are improved. And The Good Things Is and the good thing is Avm promotes a lot of such activity very much.

Educational Tours

Educational tours play an important role in educating students in a practical and interesting manner. Something that is seen in real can be easily perceived than something that we simply read in book. So taking students to trips that will relate to their academics can help them learn much better. We Organise such trips, on regular basis.

Various Quizzes, Competitions & More

The objective of every Indian school quizzing competition is to encourage students to look beyond their textual knowledge and establish a relationship between theory and application of the learnt concepts. It awakens the spirit of competition in the students' minds. We at AVM, organise various kind of quizzes and competitions every month.

Student Gatherings & Discussions

Such discussions among students, help them by training them to discuss and argue about a particular topic, it help students to express their views on serious subjects and in formal situations. It improves their thinking, listening and speaking skills. Every saturday, we held such discussion events (generally called "Bal Sabha") in school. Along with this, we invite Public figures in school, who shares their various experiences with students and motivates them.
