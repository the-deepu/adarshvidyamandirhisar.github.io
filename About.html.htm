Adarsh Vidya Mandir School

This Educational Institute Is Established in the Revolutionary period with the aim to provide the most basic requirement EDUCATION . To fulfill the purpose of Education which has always been "TO GIVE EVERY ONE,IN ESSENCE,THE SAME, TO GIVE THE YOUNG THE THINGS THEY NEED IN ORDER TO DEVELOP IN AN ORDERLY SEQUENTIAL WAY INTO MEMBERS OF SOCIETY. Main purpose of School is to provide education and basic moral values and build inner strength and confidence to children belongs to rural communities. A.V.M. is also similar to other education resources but it's familiar environment and care and motivation for whole life to all students,familiar relatedness of all the staff and students and the mentor advises to all even after passing out from school makes it unique and different from others. It's motive is always to encourages childrens for find a right path for their upcoming life and make them capable to learn how to find the beauty of life and in being responsible creature of the society. Here a child comes like a green bud and A.V.M. turns him/her into beautiful flowers for spreading the fragrance of knowledge and humanity.
